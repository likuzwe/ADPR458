Backyard

The Restaurant

The Backyard offers a journey into local barbecue. With highly trained and experience staff, dining at the Backyard is an experience. with an impeccable atmosphere that caters to people of all ages.

Services

Besides the food, Backyard offers other experiences like video games, board games, sand volleyball, pool table, ping pong and many other fun and engaging services.

Location and Hours

Gasabo, Kigali - Rwanda; Monday through Thursday 11am to 10pm; Friday and Saturday, 11am to 2.00pm
